Privacy Policy for Estimate Wars

Last updated: 30 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Estimate Wars app ("the app", "we", "us") collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using the app you agree to this policy.

Who we are

Estimate Wars is developed by Manuel Nikolov. For any privacy questions or requests, contact: manuelnikolov05@gmail.com.

Information we collect

We keep data collection to the minimum needed to run the game's online features (leaderboards, friends, and head-to-head "Wars").

  • Account identifier. When you first open the app we create an anonymous account through Firebase Authentication. This gives your device a random user ID. If you sign in with Apple, Google, or email and password, we also receive an account identifier and your email address so we can sign you in across devices. We never receive your Apple or Google password.
  • Display name. The name you set is shown to other players on leaderboards and in Wars. You can change it at any time in the app.
  • Game progress and scores. Your scores, streak, daily results, friends list, and a backup of your in-app progress are stored so they sync across your devices and appear on leaderboards.
  • Friend connections. If you add a friend by code, we store the link between your account and theirs so the friends leaderboard and Wars work.

Beyond the email address tied to your sign-in, we do not collect your contacts, your location, or any advertising identifiers. We do not show ads.

How your information is used

  • To run online leaderboards and show your rank.
  • To let you add friends and challenge players to Wars.
  • To back up and restore your game progress across devices.
  • To operate in-app purchases (handled by Apple; see below).

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising or tracking across other apps.

Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details. Apple's handling of that information is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

Third-party services

The app uses Google Firebase (Authentication and Cloud Firestore) to provide its online features. Data described above is stored on Google's servers on our behalf. See Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data retention and deletion

Your data is kept while your account exists. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app (Settings → Account → Delete Account), or by emailing manuelnikolov05@gmail.com. We will delete it within a reasonable time, except where we must keep records to comply with law.

Children

Estimate Wars is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will remove it.

Security

We rely on Google Firebase's security infrastructure and access rules to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to safeguard your information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, noted in the app.
